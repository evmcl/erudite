Erudite

Erudite will download articles from Instapaper, convert them to an
appropriate format such as EPUB or MOBI, and add them to your Calibre
ebook library. It is a command-line driven tool.

  Home Page: http://evanmclean.com/software/erudite/
  Downloads: https://github.com/evmcl/erudite/downloads
  GitHub:    https://github.com/evmcl/erudite

See doc/index.html for documentation.

See also:

  Instapaper: http://www.instapaper.com/
  Calibre:    http://calibre-ebook.com/
