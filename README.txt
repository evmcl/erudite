Erudite

Erudite will download articles from Instapaper, Readability, or Pocket,
convert them to an appropriate format such as EPUB or MOBI, and add them
to your Calibre ebook library. It is a command-line driven tool.

  Home Page: http://evanmclean.com/software/erudite/
  Downloads: https://code.google.com/p/evmcl/downloads/list
  GitHub:    https://github.com/evmcl/erudite

See doc/index.html for documentation.
